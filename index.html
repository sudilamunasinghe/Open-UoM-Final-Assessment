Who We Are?


SM TECH SL is innovation, growth and success. Our state-of-the-art tech solutions and marketing strategies propel businesses beyond their competitors into a league of their own.

We are a digital agency based out of Matara, Sri Lanka. Since our inception in 2021, we have delivered only the best customized digital solutions resulting in successful businesses that have kept growing.

We have an expert team of web developers, graphic designers and content creators dedicated to creating bespoke strategic enterprise web and mobile solutions for any business. At SM TECH SL, we deliver high-quality products and services that are affordable and fit for any business, be it a start-up or a well-developed company.

Our Mission

Working with clients who love their work combines into a fun, wonderful partnership for everyone involved.
